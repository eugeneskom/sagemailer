Steps for set up

Buyer-Seller Messaging

You can send the review requests with the Buyer-Seller Messaging system in your Seller Central account. This feature allows sellers to customize their messages.

However, it's necessary to follow all the platform's guidelines for communication with buyers. So, it’s better to check the policies on how to get good reviews on Amazon before sending direct messages.

Request a Review Button

In this case, Amazon sends the message with a review request by itself on behalf of the seller upon their request. The seller can’t change or customize the message.

However, it's automatically translated into the customer's language of preference. You can send requests manually from Order Details in your Seller Central account or use third-party software to automate the whole process. Amazon sellers have been receiving outstanding results with this type of messaging. After implementing this tool, recent research among Amazon customers showed a 41% increase in the average review rate per day.
